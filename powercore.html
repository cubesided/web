Powercore is a web browser built in C# with a help by CefSharp.
Designed in winforms, .NET Framework, & Visual Studio 2022

The idea of Powercore is to bring back customization in browsers.
Trapezoidal tabs, Internet Explorer visual style, and more.

Currently in its ALPHA stage. Visit github to find out more!
